Agentry {{ .Chart.AppVersion }} installed into namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }}.

The chart installs:
  - the 5 CustomResourceDefinitions
  - the controller and gateway Deployments (2 replicas each, PDB minAvailable 1)
  - ServiceAccounts, ClusterRoles/Bindings, and the gateway's namespaced Role
  - the cert-manager trust chain (agentry-selfsigned + agentry-ca-issuer
    ClusterIssuers, the agentry-ca root Certificate, the gateway and controller
    leaf Certificates) and the trust-manager agentry-ca Bundle
  - a default "standard" AgentClass

Prerequisites (the chart does NOT install these):
  1. cert-manager, running with --enable-certificate-owner-ref=true
  2. trust-manager, with --trust-namespace matching
     certManager.clusterResourceNamespace (default "cert-manager")
  3. a NetworkPolicy-enforcing CNI (kindnet/flannel do not qualify)

Check the rollout:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} rollout status deploy/agentry-controller
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} rollout status deploy/agentry-gateway

The Deployments mount cert-manager-issued Secrets and the trust-manager CA
ConfigMap; on first install the Pods stay ContainerCreating until those issue.
If they never become Ready, verify the agentry-ca-issuer ClusterIssuer is
Ready and that certManager.clusterResourceNamespace matches your cert-manager
and trust-manager configuration.

CRD upgrades are never applied by `helm upgrade`. Before upgrading, run:
  kubectl apply --server-side -f charts/agentry/crds/
